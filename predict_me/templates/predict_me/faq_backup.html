What is Predict Me?
Predict Me is focused on providing individual donation prediction based on the historical data. Predict Me goal is to analyze historical individual donor giving trends, demographic pattern, educational level and other important variables using Machine Learning and Predictive Modeling techniques to increase the likelihood of future donations for nonprofit organizations. Any nonprofit that accepts individual donations and has an individual as a donor can use this tool to predict donors who will donate in the future.
How do you help?
PM aim to create a solution where an end-user can upload a dataset of donors and non-donors, and our application pre-process the dataset, and then train multiple different classification algorithms/models on that data. These models then can be used to predict past and new records of donors, provided that the record is in the same setting or format as the initial dataset was. For example, if the dataset provided by the user had two columns named, ‘Age’, ‘Donation Value’, then the record passed into the model/code for prediction should also contain this information.
Can Predictive Modeling drive funding?
Organization, no matter big or small are always on the hunt to figure out the best way to achieve their fundraising goals in a very cost-efficient manner. Contacting the right donor, frequency of contact, how much to ask to achieve the fundraising goals are some of the aspects that is made easier with the help of Predict Me. Donors are like a customer to a business. They can be a loyal and repetitive donor or a onetime donor, they could provide funding either big or small. Based on the accurate modeling, Predict Me will be able to understand donor’s behavior using the exact same methodologies a for profit uses to track their customer’s behavior.

How does monthly subscription and data limit work?
Your monthly subscription cycle starts from the day you start your plan and ends after 30 days. It will auto renew after 30 days. During the 30 days period, you can either use all your data limit on the day you sign up to run your predictive model or use the data in batches until you hit your data limit. For example, you start your subscription model on January 1st and you selected 200 data limits per month. Based on the data limit, you can run the entire data (total of 200) on the day you sign up or within 30 days or you could run them in batches. For example, you could run first 50 data records on the 1st day and 100 data records on the 5th day and remainder 50 data records on the 30th day. It really depends on how you use it.
Will my un-used data limit carry over to the next month?
No. You will have to ensure to use and run all your data limit within that 30 days period. If you continue to have your monthly subscription plan, your data limit will auto reset. If you have any unused data, you will not be able to carry that over to the next cycle.
Will I get refund for my un-used data limit that cost me dollar per record?
No. There is no refund policy for unused data. We highly recommend you use and run all your data limit within that 30 days period to maximize your return on investment. Please refer to our Refund Policy and Payment Terms (hyperlink this to Payment terms from FAQ) here.
I just used up all my data limit before 30 days period. I need to run predict model on a new donor file. What should I do? Wil I be charged again for the new file?
It’s simple and Yes! No matter which subscription plan you already have, you have two options. Option 1: Wait until the next monthly cycle to start and use the new donor file to run the prediction model in the next cycle. Option 2: Use custom cost option to run a new donor file. For example, if you used all your 200 data limits and you want to run the new file, simply upload the file, choose the one-time option and run the model. Just a reminder that this option pricing is different than your monthly plan. Follow the process. Good luck!
What is the cost to run “one-time” option?
There are two types of cost for the “one-time” option. The cost to run one-time model is $500 which will give you all 5 best fit models and cost per record is $2. For example, if your new file has 1,000 records, it will be charged as follows. Model Cost: $500 Cost Per Record: $2 Total Cost $2,500 ($500 + $2 X 1,000 records)
Will this one-time model cost change anything on my monthly subscription plan?
No. Your monthly subscription plan will remain unchanged will still be active. Since this is run on a “one-time” basis, it won’t have any changes on your regular plan. This is only done when required and will be charged separately.
Will I have access to my results and profiles even though my subscription ended?
No. Your donor prediction results and profiles will only remain active until the valid subscription period. If your subscription ended or you cancelled, you will have to renew your subscription to gain access to the results and profile.
